Monthly active contributors

20% Pull request creation : Measures active contributors
- We use PR's only in order to minimize outliers caused by an inconsistencies on how repos enforce squashing.
- This has the lowest weight in order to minimize outliers due to some PR's being made by bots. 

80% Issue engagement: Mesures active engagement
- We use comments on issues as a way to detect active engagement, this minimizes outliers.
- Our process enforces issue creation in order to work on a feature, therefore this would be a more accurate indicative of active engagement that minimizes outliers.



Measures adoption and interest growth

20% FCOT – Fork count over time
Measures adoption growth by calculating the forks made per quarter.

80% SGOT – Star growth over time
Measures interest growth by calculating stars made per quarter.
